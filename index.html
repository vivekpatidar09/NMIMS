    ABOUT NMIMS CET (NCET)

    The NMIMS Common Entrance Test (NCET) is the university-level examination to Undergraduate Engineering and Pharmacy degree programs. These programs are offered at NMIMS campuses in Mumbai, Indore, Navi Mumbai, Shirpur, Chandigarh, and Hyderabad.

    SCHOOLS COVERED UNDER NMIMS NCET

    Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management and Engineering (MPSTME) and School of Technology and Management (STME)

    Offers diverse undergraduate programs like B Tech in collaboration with VT, USA, B Tech, B Tech in collaboration with TCS along with its niche integrated dual degree program B Tech + MBA (MBA Tech).

    Shobhaben Pratapbhai Patel School of Pharmacy & Technology (SPPSPTM) and School of Pharmacy & Technology (SPTM)

    Offers a unique one of its kind, a dual degree program B Pharm + MBA (Pharma Tech). Acknowledged for its excellence, the school holds the 11th rank in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) .
